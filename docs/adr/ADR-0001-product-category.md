# ADR-0001 — Edifico Product Category

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

Edifico includes workforce, projects, Gantt, contract controls, BIM, documents, reports, tenants, and other modules. Without a clear category, the product can become a broad collection of construction tools.

## Decision

Edifico is a **Construction Operating System**.

## Consequences

- Execution workflows receive priority over pure BIM features.
- BIM remains a context layer.
- Gantt and planning must connect to execution.
- Reporting must measure execution truth.
- AI is deferred until operational data is clean.

## Rejected alternatives

- BIM platform.
- Workforce-only system.
- Generic project management system.
- CDE-only system.
